🎧 Live Mass Interpreter

Listen to the Mass in English, live — for our brothers and sisters from Ghana

Idle

Put on your earphones. Hold the phone's microphone toward the priest/speakers. You'll hear the Mass translated in your ear — fixed parts (greeting, prayers, responses) are spoken instantly; readings and the homily are translated with a small delay, like simultaneous interpretation.

Debug: what the app is hearing (Portuguese)

…

This app listens to the priest's spoken Portuguese, speaks the known parts of the Mass in English right away, and translates+speaks the readings/homily with a short delay — so you can follow along by ear, like everyone else.

Needs microphone permission, works best in Chrome, and use earphones so the translated audio doesn't loop back into the microphone.
